About Me

Welcome! This is the personal page of Tengjiao He. I graduated from University of Wollongong, Australia, with Honors. I then obtained my Ph.D. degree under the supervision of A/Prof Kwan-Wu Chin. I am currently a lecturer at College of Information Science and Technology, Jinan University.

My Research Areas

  • Integrated Data and Energy Communication Network.

  • Network wide resource allocation for 5G networks, IoT, MIMO and NOMA.

  • Applications of optimization theory and machine learning in communication systems.
